How we build

Principles, not platitudes

These aren't aspirations — they're constraints we hold ourselves to in every product decision.

🏗️

Built to last

We optimize for durability over speed. Every architectural decision is made with a ten-year horizon, not a next-quarter growth target.

🔍

Transparent by default

Our algorithms, pricing, and data practices are documented and explained. If we can't defend a decision in plain language, we don't ship it.

🚫

No dark patterns

We don't manufacture urgency, bury cancellation flows, or exploit psychological vulnerabilities. Honest design is non-negotiable.

🌱

Sustainable growth

We grow by being genuinely useful. Retention through value, not addiction. We'd rather have fewer users who trust us than millions who don't.

About SaaSquatch Lab

Independent software, long-term thinking

SaaSquatch Lab is an independent software company based in the Pacific Northwest. We build tools for communities, job seekers, and civic organizations — things that solve real problems without surveillance-based business models.

Every product we ship is self-funded and built on clear principles. We answer to our users, not investors. That means we can build for the long run.
